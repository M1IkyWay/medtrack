MedTrack — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 July 2026

MedTrack is a privacy-first medication reminder. This policy explains, in plain language, what the app does and does not do with your information. The short version: MedTrack does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

Summary — No accounts. No servers. No analytics. No tracking. No ads. Everything you enter stays on your device.

Information we collect

None. MedTrack has no user accounts and no backend. The medications, schedules, and dose history you enter are stored only in a local database on your device.

How your data is stored

All app data is kept locally on your device using an on-device database. It is never uploaded to us or to any third party. If you delete the app, that data is removed with it.

Notifications

MedTrack schedules local reminders on your device. These notifications are generated and delivered entirely on the device and are not sent through any external service.

Analytics and third parties

MedTrack contains no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no third-party trackers. The app makes no network requests to transmit your data.

Children's privacy

MedTrack does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children, because it does not collect data at all.

Medical disclaimer

MedTrack is a reminder tool and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always follow the instructions of your doctor or pharmacist.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "last updated" date.
